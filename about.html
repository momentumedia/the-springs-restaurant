About

A restored church, a room for your day, and the people who keep it running.

The Springs is an event venue and a farm-to-table restaurant on Springbank Drive in London, Ontario. The building was a church. The kitchen is ours.

Most of what happens here is a gathering that needs a room. Weddings, bridal showers, birthdays, corporate dinners, pharma education evenings, celebrations of life, communions, retirements. We also run an a-la-carte dining room on most nights of the week.

However you find us, the approach is the same. Quiet when you want quiet, lively when you want lively, never stiff.

The room

A building worth restoring, restored.

Arched windows, exposed brick, pitched ceilings, real wood floors. The church's bones shape the room. Dinner service feels different because of it. Weddings in particular feel like they belong.

Complimentary parking is on site. The main entrance is wheelchair accessible. We'll find you the table that fits the night you're having.

Sourcing

Farm to table, meant literally.

Our menu changes with what's in season and what's coming in from nearby farms. We don't print a list in January and pretend it applies in July.

The kitchen works with what the growers have. If something runs out, something else takes its place. The result is a list that reads a little differently every few weeks, and a plate that tastes like the month you're in.

Events

We host the days people remember.

Weddings under the peaked ceiling. Corporate and pharmaceutical dinners in the private room. Bridal showers on long tables under white florals. Celebrations of life for families who need a quiet place and warm food. Birthdays, retirements, graduations, communions, baptisms, engagement parties, bachelor parties. We've hosted all of it.
