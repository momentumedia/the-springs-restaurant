Why The Springs

Your event, your way. No rule book.

We built the business around the simple idea that a venue should get out of a host's way. If you haven't seen your event type listed on the site, we still host it. Book the date and we'll shape the day around what you have in mind.

  • Any hour

    Mornings, nights, holidays, middle of the night. Pick the time, we'll open.

  • No venue fee

    Zero rental cost. Pay for food and drink, nothing else.

  • No deposit

    No money upfront to hold a date.

  • No minimum spend

    Eight guests or eighty. A proposal or a fundraiser. No floor.

  • No restrictions

How it works

Book first. Shape the day second.

  1. Book a date. Any day, any hour. Through our Square appointments page.
  2. Pick the room. Private room for 35, main hall for 85, outdoor tent for more.

FAQ

Quick answers.

  • What if my event type isn't listed on your site?

    Book it anyway. The categories cover what we host most often, not what we're limited to.

  • Can we host a fundraiser or non-profit event?

    Yes. Silent auctions, benefits, non-profit mixers. The hall reads serious when you need it to, celebratory when you want that.

  • Do you host cultural or religious events?

    Yes. Quinceañeras, bar and bat mitzvahs, religious holidays, any cultural celebration. Bring your ceremony and your traditions. We adapt.

  • Can we host a class reunion?

    Yes. Private room for smaller gatherings, main hall for full-year turnouts.

  • Is there a venue fee or deposit?

    No. No venue fee. No deposit. No minimum spend. No restrictions on event type.

  • What hours?

    Any hour. Including holidays and overnight.

  • Is the venue accessible?

    Ground-level entry, wheelchair accessible, complimentary on-site parking.
