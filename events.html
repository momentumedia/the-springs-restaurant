FAQ

Quick answers.

If the question isn't here, call or send a note.

  • Is there a venue fee?

    No. There's no rental fee. You pay for food and drink. The room is yours.

  • Do you require a deposit?

    No deposit. Confirm your date and we put it on the calendar.

  • Is there a minimum spend or minimum guest count?

    No minimum spend. No minimum head count. Plan for eight or eighty. Both work.

  • Are there restrictions on the type of event we can host?

    No. Any event type, any format, any decor, any music. Bring your vision. We don't hand you a rule book.

  • What hours can we book for an event?

    Any hour. Mornings, afternoons, evenings, overnight, holidays. Our dining-room hours apply to walk-in meals, not to private events.

  • How do we book?

    Book directly through our Square appointments page. That's the fastest way to confirm your date. Once the date is in, we follow up with menu options and logistics.

  • What's the capacity?

    The main hall seats up to 85 for a plated dinner. The private room holds up to 35. The outdoor tent extends capacity well beyond 85 for larger receptions.

  • Is the venue accessible?

    Yes. Ground-level entry, wheelchair accessible, complimentary on-site parking.
