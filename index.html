Why The Springs

Booking an event should not feel like a negotiation.

We don't charge a venue fee. We don't require a deposit. We don't hold you to a minimum spend. We don't restrict what kind of event you can throw. And we'll open the doors at any hour, any day, including holidays.

  • Any hour

    Morning showers. Late-night receptions. Holidays. Pick a time that works for you and we'll be open.

  • No venue fee

    Zero rental cost. The room is yours for the price of food and drink, nothing more.

  • No deposit

    We don't ask for money up front to hold your date. Just confirm and we're on the calendar.

  • No minimum spend

    Pay for exactly what you plan. A small room for eight or a wedding for eighty, the number lands where it lands.

  • No restrictions

    Any type of event. Bring your own decor, your own music, your own format. We don't hand you a rule book.

The building

A century-old church, restored and set for your day.

Arched windows, exposed brick, pitched wood ceilings, soft light through stained glass. We restored the bones carefully, then turned it into the kind of room people remember.

Main hall seats 85. Private room for 35. An outdoor tent for receptions that outgrow the building. The kitchen runs farm-to-table and the service team has done every kind of day under these ceilings.
