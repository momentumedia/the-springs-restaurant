The building

A century-old church, set for dinner.

The bones of a church. Arched windows, exposed brick, pitched ceilings, wooden floors. We restored it carefully and turned it into a dining room.

We cook with what's in season and what grows close to home. The menu reads plainly because the sourcing speaks for itself. Come for a weeknight dinner, book the room for a wedding, or set a long table for the people you love.
