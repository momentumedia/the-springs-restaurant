Why The Springs

No fee. No deposit. No minimum. No rule book. Any hour.

Most wedding venues charge for the room. We don't. We don't ask for a deposit to hold the date. We don't set a minimum spend or a minimum head count. We don't restrict what the day looks like. And we'll open at any hour. These aren't loopholes. They're how we run the building.

  • Any hour

    Morning ceremony. Afternoon reception. Late-night after-party. Holidays included.

  • No venue fee

    The room is included in the per-person price. No rental charge on top.

  • No deposit

    We won't hold your credit card to hold the date. A date is a date.

  • No minimum spend

    Small intimate wedding for twenty. Full hall for eighty. No floor.

  • No restrictions

    Your vendors, your decor, your music, your timeline. Your day, your way.

Wedding FAQ

Quick answers, specific to weddings.

  • How many guests can you hold for a wedding?

    Up to 85 seated in the main hall. The outdoor tent extends capacity beyond that for larger receptions.

  • Is there a venue rental fee?

    No. There's no venue fee. You pay for food and drink. The room is included.

  • Do we pay a deposit to hold the date?

    No deposit required. Confirm the date and it's yours.

  • Can we host the ceremony and reception at the same place?

    Yes. Ceremony in the main hall or under the tent, reception in the main hall. No bus transfers, no hauling.

  • What's dinner per person?

    Three dinner set-menu tiers: $79 (Deluxe), $99 (Deluxe Plus), $115 (Premium). Every price is on the set menus page. Lunch tiers start at $65.

  • Do you host bridal showers and wedding showers?

    Yes, regularly. Private room up to 35, or main hall for larger shower parties. Custom menu design available.

  • Is the venue accessible?

    Ground-level entry, wheelchair accessible, complimentary on-site parking.
