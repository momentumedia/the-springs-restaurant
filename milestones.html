Why The Springs

The room you want, on the day you want it.

We don't charge a venue fee. We don't ask for a deposit. We don't set a minimum spend or a minimum head count. We don't restrict what your milestone can look like. And we'll open any hour. Book a single table for a proposal or the full hall for a ninetieth birthday.

  • Any hour

    Morning baptisms. Afternoon communions. Evening birthdays. Late-night bachelor parties.

  • No venue fee

    Zero rental charge. Food and drink, that's it.

  • No deposit

    Confirm the date and you're on the calendar.

  • No minimum

    Six guests or eighty. Proposal for two. Full hall retirement. No floor.

  • No restrictions

    Your decor, your music, your format. Speeches, slideshows, piñatas. Your milestone, your rules.

Birthdays

For the round-number years. And all the others.

First birthdays, thirtieths, fiftieths, eightieths, ninetieths. A single long table with close family. A full hall with everyone you've ever known. Candles on the cake. Speeches or no speeches. No minimum.

  • Any guest count, any age
  • Private room (35) or main hall (85)
  • Custom menus for dietary needs
  • Extended hours for late parties
  • Children and wheelchair friendly
Engagements · proposals · bachelor

The before, the during, the after.

A single table in the private room set for two for a proposal. The main hall for an engagement party. A louder send-off under the tent for the bachelor group. Short notice works. After-hours works.

  • Proposal: a single table for two, after-hours available
  • Engagement party: main hall or private room
  • Bachelor or bachelorette: main hall or outdoor tent
  • Hors-d'oeuvre service for receptions
Graduations

The day after the ceremony, or right after the walk.

High-school, undergrad, grad-school, professional-school graduations. Private room for immediate family, main hall for extended family or classmates. Lunch or dinner. Any day of the week.

  • Private room (35) for family dinners
  • Main hall (85) for bigger celebrations
  • Lunch set menus from $65 per person
  • Open to speeches and slideshows
Retirements

The years that ran long deserve a room.

Retirement dinners for careers that earned them. Colleagues, family, both. Private room for intimate, main hall for larger groups, hors-d'oeuvre reception if you'd rather mingle. Speeches land well in this acoustic.

  • Private room or main hall
  • Hors-d'oeuvre, lunch, or dinner formats
  • Mic and AV on request
Communions · baptisms · confirmations

A post-service lunch in a room that used to be a church.

First communions, baptisms, confirmations. The building was a church and the room still reflects that. Sunday-lunch service after the ceremony, or a quiet evening gathering. Child-friendly menu options.

  • Child-friendly menus and service
  • Daytime and weekend availability
  • Ground-level entry for strollers and grandparents
  • Quiet private room for close family
Anniversaries · reunions · anything else

  • Any format, any size

How it works

Plain. No runaround.

  1. Pick a date. Any day, any hour. Holidays included.
  2. Pick a room. Private room for 35, main hall for 85, or both.
  3. Pick a menu. Seven published set menus. Or build something custom.
  4. Book. Through our Square appointments page. We follow up with menu options and logistics after.
  5. Show up. No deposit. No venue fee. No minimum. Just the day itself.

Milestone FAQ

Quick answers.

  • Do you host milestone birthdays?

    Yes. First through ninetieth. No minimum guest count.

  • Can we host an engagement party or private proposal?

    Yes. Single table for two for a proposal, or a full engagement party. After-hours availability for private proposals.

  • Do you do small graduation dinners?

    Yes. Private room for family, main hall for extended. Lunch or dinner.

  • Do you host communions and baptisms?

    Yes. Post-service lunches are common here. Child-friendly menus.

  • What's the minimum guest count?

    No minimum. Plan for six or eighty.

  • What are your event hours?

    Any hour. Private events run independent of our walk-in dining-room schedule.

  • Is the venue accessible?

    Ground-level entry, wheelchair accessible, complimentary on-site parking.
